Information Overload 2012-05-20

May 20th, 2012

  • Hot spring yields hybrid genome
    This is about a month old, but I finally got around to reading it. Also: a combined RNA+DNA virus, damn.
  • Kodak Had A Secret Nuclear Reactor Loaded With Enriched Uranium Hidden In A Basement
    While “they could have started their own nuclear war if they wanted” is a terrible exaggeration, this is still interesting.
  • Who wants to buy Honduras
    I’m not sure what to think of this proposed “charter city”.
  • The Bizarre History of the Vibrator: From Cleopatra’s Angry Bees to Steam-Powered Dildos
    A Gizmondo article on a different kind of gadget.

    Since this is from Cracked.com it’s rather light-hearted, but still something you may want to send to people who are too stuck on certain gender roles.

    I’ve also followed this “own less” approach for a few years now, it’s quite liberating.

    Interesting. Also: coolest judge ever?

    A very nice article on Kurt Gödel.
  • Google goes cancer: Researchers use search engine algorithm to find cancer biomarkers
    Nice work by TU Dresden.
  • Why Aren’t Cities Littered With Dead Pigeons?
    This is the sort of question that really needs an answer.
