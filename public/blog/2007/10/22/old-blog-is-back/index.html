Old Blog Is Back

Oct 22nd, 2007

Don’t get me wrong, Soup really is great! But it’s just not the tool for writing longer posts, and I’m missing the opportunity to get comments on my musings. So here’s how I’ll handle things from now on:


  • citizen428.soup: The whole package, including tumblelog, Flickr pics, del.icio.us bookmarks and my blog.

  • citizen428.blog: Giving in to popular demand (viz. two people) this is the no frills, blog posts only side of me. I’ve also created a different RSS feed for this, so you don’t have to go through loads of pics and URLs if you are only interested in my ramblings.
