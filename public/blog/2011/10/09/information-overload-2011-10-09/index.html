Information Overload 2011-10-09

Oct 9th, 2011

Thus, tweeting "Down with capitalism" from your iPhone for those around the world to read seems to be the most ironic thing a person can do.

Robert David Graham Independent Reporting of #OccupyWallStreet
  • What it costs to live in Japan
    A foreigner living in Japan gives an interesting breakdown of the cost of living there.
  • Belgian ISPs Ordered To Block The Pirate Bay
    Two Belgian ISPs received a court order to block the Pirate Bay after the Belgian Anti-Piracy foundation won a court appeal.
  • The Life-Changing $20 Rightward-Facing Cow
    Game designer, lecturer and author Ian Bogost was so bothered by “social gaming” and “gamification”, that he bothered to release a satire game, which turned out become his biggest success. Great read!
  • Brazil Drafts An ‘Anti-ACTA’: A Civil Rights-Based Framework For The Internet
    After already being big on FOSS and open content use, Brazil now also pushes for a new Internet regulation, that actually has a strong civil rights focus.
  • The Plan to Bring an Asteroid to Earth
    I love when science starts sounding like science fiction.
  • Destruktive Dynamik im Handelsraum
    A study found that financial traders act more ruthless than psychopaths. Who’d have thought…. (in German)
  • Vladimir Putin, Action Man
    Vladimir Putin, the James Bond of politics. Joking aside though, this photo series is a great summary of the public image he has created in recent years.
  • Independent reporting of #OccupyWallStreet
    An insightful article about the current protests, with a fair share of media criticism and good observations about the protesters. I often find myself liking things written by people who label themselves libertarians, but it still is a philosophy I have strong doubts about.
  • Computer virus hits US Predator and Reaper drone fleet
    A hard to remove virus on military infrastructure and once again it seems like external storage media was the culprit.
  • Hipster: The Dead End of Western Civilization
    This article on hipsterdom feels so contemporary, I hadn’t even noticed that it’s more than 3 years old until I finished it.

If only we carried rocks instead of cameras, we’d look like revolutionaries.

Douglas Haddow Hipster: The Dead End of Western Civilization
